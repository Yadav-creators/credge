Why InterEdge?

Student-centric

Internships are tailor-made to fit the wants, needs and abilities of students.

Custom-made internships

Guaranteed custom-made internships for students in partnership with our list of employers; from start-ups to international corporations.

Personalised tasks

Vocational preparedness

Providing necessary skills for students to succeed in their internships as well as their schooling in the future and other work experiences.

Assessment and Feedback
