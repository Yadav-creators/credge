Why Research edge?

ResearchEdge helps students conduct research in an online and offline environment thus bringing out their creative and research skills in an engaging and thoughtful manner.

Students can explore their academic interests under the guidance of professors from leading universities in the US and India and mentors from global industries.

Our research programs use innovative data analytics tools and research techniques and methodologies to support evidence based decision making, solve important research questions and nurture the spirit of creativity and innovation in students.

ResearchEdge brings together talented, passionate and dedicated students as they strive to make discoveries that have a real impact on lives and societies across the world

About program

At ResearchEdge, we offer two distinct programs:

  • Research Scholar Program
  • Achiever Program

Research Scholar Program

  • The Research Scholar Program provides high school students with a unique and personalized opportunity to conduct research in an online and offline environment under the mentorship of leading professors, scientists and other subject experts from various universities in India and the US.
  • It allows students to experience first-hand how research is carried out and to contribute consequentially.
  • Students in the Research Scholar Program will learn fundamental research techniques through a combination of interactive lectures and stimulating coursework culminating in a research paper which can be published in leading international journals.
  • Students will be able to publish their research in an academic journal.

Achiever Program

  • The Achiever Program is a highly intensive program for high school and undergraduate students to begin their research work in their field of interest.
  • t immerses students in college-level research work thereby placing them on the right track for careers in research and innovation.
  • Students will develop an understanding of research through exposure to research fields such as education, medicine, psychology, engineering, sustainability etc.
  • Students can publish their research in an academic journal.
  • This is a highly selective program and acceptance into this program requires students to have a high GPA. Acceptance rate into this program is around 10%

Why ResearchEdge

  • We are one of the handful of research organizations which offer both online and offline research programs.
  • We are the only program which has a team consisting of both US and India professors and mentors from leading universities
  • We empower students to Research, Create, Explore, Innovate and Learn while experiencing sheer joy during the process.
  • We encourage students to challenge the Status Quo.
  • Completing a ResearchEdge Program will give students a credential testifying their ability to complete college-level work
  • For international students, completing the program gives them another way to tell the admissions committee that they are prepared to study in the US
  • The ResearchEdge program will help students complete an undergraduate level research paper
